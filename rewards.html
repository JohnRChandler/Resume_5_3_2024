Hustle Cup Point System

  1. Earning Points:

    Customers earn points for every dollar spent on purchases at HCup. The rate at which points are earned increases as customers progress through our tiered loyalty program. Basic members earn 1 point per dollar, Premium members earn 1.5 points per dollar, and Hustle members earn 2 points per dollar spent.

  2. Redeeming Points:

    Accumulated points can be redeemed for various rewards, such as free coffees, size upgrades, exclusive access to new products, and more. The redemption rate and the rewards available may vary by membership level, enhancing the value as customers reach higher tiers.

  3. Point Expiry:

    Points have a shelf life of one year from the date they are earned, encouraging customers to use them regularly instead of hoarding them. Inactive accounts, or those without any earning or redeeming activity for a year, may see their points expire.

  4. Account Management:

    Customers can manage their points through their online HCup account, where they can check their balance, view transaction history, and manage their accounts.
